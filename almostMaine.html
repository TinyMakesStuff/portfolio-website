I don't have anything creative to say about this one it wasn't that memorable

It's Almost, Maine if you haven't guessed

So, in preparation of writing our 10-minute plays, we read the play Almost, Maine. You know, I’m actually really glad we read Almost, Maine, it taught me a lot about how to properly use the medium of theater and helped me understand the limits of what we can/can’t do. I also just enjoyed the play; it had some cute moments, and I really liked its little bits of world building which was something I would try to apply to future works.
