Now THAT was a fun brainstorming session

The screenplay treatment

Since we worked so well together last time, my twin and I teamed up with each other to write out screenplay. We were kinda struggling to come up with ideas for what we wanted to do, we kept throwing ideas at the wall but nothing would stick. Then, struck by a bolt of inspiration at the mere mention of the word heist, I came up with the idea of a heist at Chuck E. Cheese. And from there we were off, coming up with idea after idea, attempting to make each other laugh as much as possible as we created out treatment. Once we turned it in, it made me realize that writing with a partner who enjoys what your doing as much as you is a lot of fun. I was looking forward to what we would come up with next.
